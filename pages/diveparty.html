"Dive Party" is a multiplayer cooperative game where 4 players take on different roles to control a submarine. The captain has a map and controls the movement and direction of the ship, the torch lights up the way and stuns enemies, the treasurer uses a magnet to locate and collect treasure, and the cannoneer shoots at enemies with limited ammo.
Collect as much treasure as you can before the time runs out, and be careful of the octopus dwelling in the deepest part of the caverns!

This game was developed specifically for the Jam-O-Drum, a custom controller housed at the Carnegie Mellon University Entertainment Technology Center. It features a projection-mapped display with rotation & button input for each player.
