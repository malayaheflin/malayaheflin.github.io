Kill the Vampire


December 2023

team: Malaya Heflin, Sneha Patel, Jack Maloy, Garrett Amos, Aryan Shrestha

tools: Unity, C#, Maya

"Kill the Vampire" is a short game where the player must overcome three challenges to slay the vampire and save the town. First, they must adventure through the castle, using both near and far range combat to beat monsters. Then, they'll be chased through a winding maze, using beacons to distract the vampire and get away when in need of an escape. Finally, they'll end up in the castle's dungeon, where they'll solve a series of puzzles to trap the vampire once and for all.

✰ my specific contributions

I worked on the mechanics and level design for the first part of the game's puzzle segment. This includes implementing the repel/attraction of crates via player shooting, utilizing Unity raycasts to program the laser and its reflection behavior, etc. I also modeled 3D assets (crates, laser shooter and receptor) within Maya, and hand-drew some of the 2D textures.
