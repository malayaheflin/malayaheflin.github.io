The Earthkeeper


may 2024

team: Malaya Heflin, Zoe Poppert, Elaine Gao

tools: TouchDesigner, Kinect Azure, projection

“The Earthkeeper” is an interactive projection installation where users are invited to use their movement to temporarily grow a desolate forest. We hope to use our piece to demonstrate the simple beauty of regrowth. Through hand-drawn animations, TouchDesigner, and a Kinect sensor, users can manipulate the environment projected before them through their own motion.
Our piece was exhibited to the general public on May 9th 2024 at the AV Williams Building, as part of the Immersive Media Design Capstone Fair.
