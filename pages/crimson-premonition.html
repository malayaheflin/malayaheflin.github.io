"Crimson Premonition" is a short multiplayer game in which 2 players have to work together to solve a series of clues. These clues are implemented with gestural recognition (tracking body movement/position via webcam), particle systems, and audio/image manipulation. It was publicly exhibited on December 8th, 2023, at the Brendan Iribe Center, as part of the Immersive Media Design End of Year Show 2023.
