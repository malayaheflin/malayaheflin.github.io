Malaya Heflin is a creative technologist studying interactive media. Currently, she's a senior undergraduate in the computing track of the Immersive Media Design BS program, a collaboration between the University of Maryland's departments of computer science and fine arts. Her interests and goals lie in combining her love for both technology and art to create innovative and interactive spaces- including themed entertainment, video games, animation, theatrical and artistic spaces, or virtual and augmented reality.
