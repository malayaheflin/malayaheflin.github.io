Malaya Heflin is a creative technologist studying interactive media. She is a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing her Master of Entertainment Technology degree. She graduated Cum Laude in May 2024 with a BS in the Immersive Media Design program, a collaboration between the University of Maryland's departments of computer science and fine arts.

Her interests and goals lie in combining her love for both technology and art to create innovative and interactive spaces- including themed or location based entertainment, game design, animation, or virtual and augmented reality. She specializes in programming and production for entertainment technology products.
