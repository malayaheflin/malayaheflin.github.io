✰ about me

I'm a senior undergraduate in the computing track of the Immersive Media Design BS program, a collaboration between the University of Maryland's departments of computer science and fine arts. My goal is to combine my love for both technology and art to create innovative and interactive spaces- including themed entertainment, video games, animation, theatrical and artistic spaces, or virtual and augmented reality.

A virtual reality animation that puts the viewer in the middle of Donald Duck's premiere short, "The Wise Little Hen"
